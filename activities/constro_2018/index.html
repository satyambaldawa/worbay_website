CONSTRO 2018

Constro International Fair, flagship event of PCERF

Event Date 18th to 21st January 2018


Constro International Fair, flagship event of PCERF started in the year 1985, is now credited amongst the largest event in the Infrastructure & Civil Engineering industry in India.Constro 2018 15th edition, is an inclusive International Fair that exhibits Construction Machinery, Materials, Methods & Projects in Pune, This Exhibition has also been recognized by ITPO New Delhi, supported by reputed industry associations, academic institutes and participated by hundreds of companies from India and few from abroad.OM SAI FIRE SYSTEMS has participated in this event between 18th Jan 2018 to 21st Jan 2018. We got such a biggest platform to represent our work & services towards society. Also we got very good response and best deals. We learnt a lot from this event.
